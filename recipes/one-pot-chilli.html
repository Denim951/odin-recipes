One Pot Chilli Recipe

Photo by stephanie monfette on Unsplash

Description

This easy vegan chili recipe is made with canned beans for a fast, convenient plant-based meal. You’ll only need one pot and 30 minutes to make this cozy dinner. The most effort required is when you chop the veggies. It’s full of bold flavor, easy to make with simple pantry staples, and packed with healthy fiber.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) Kidney beans or black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can (14 oz) Diced tomatoes
  • 1 Medium onion and 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 Bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons Tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • Chili powder – This is the main seasoning blend in this recipe! You can also add cumin and oregano.
  • Salt, ground black pepper, and a pinch of cayenne for heat
  • Cilantro or avocado for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the Oil: In a deep pot over medium-high heat, add the olive oil.
  2. Add in the Onions and Peppers: Cook for about 5 minutes until they start to soften up.
  3. Add the Garlic and Spices: Stir in the garlic, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper, letting them toast for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Mix in the Tomatoes and Paste: Pour in the diced tomatoes and tomato paste, stirring well to combine everything.
  5. Add in the Beans and Stir Again: Toss in your drained beans and about half a cup of water or vegetable broth.
  6. Bring the chili to a boil, and then reduce to medium-low so that the mixture continues to simmer for 15 to 20 more minutes to let the flavors blend.
  7. Serve and Enjoy with toppings: Remove the chili from the heat, ladle it into bowls, and top it with fresh cilantro or avocado.
