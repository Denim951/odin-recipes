Chocolate Pudding Recipe

Photo by American Heritage Chocolate on Unsplash

Description

This easy chocolate pudding recipe is made with ripe avocados for a fast, convenient vegan dessert. You’ll only need one blender and 10 minutes to make this sweet treat. The most effort required is when you scoop out the ingredients. It’s full of rich chocolate flavor, easy to make with simple ingredients, and packed with healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large ripe avocados, pitted and scooped out
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa powder or cacao powder
  • 1/2 cup Maple syrup – This is the main sweetener blend in this recipe! You can also use agave nectar.
  • 1/3 cup Plant milk (like almond or oat milk) to help it blend smoothly
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt to balance the sweetness
  • Berries or dairy-free chocolate chips for topping

Steps

  1. Prep the Base: In a food processor or high-speed blender, add the scooped avocado flesh.
  2. Add in the Sweetener and Cocoa: Pour the maple syrup, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt over the avocados.
  3. Pour in the Milk: Start by adding a splash of your plant milk to help the blades catch the ingredients.
  4. Blend the Mixture: Turn on the blender and process on high until the mixture is completely smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes
  5. Adjust the Texture and Taste: If the pudding is too thick, add a little more plant milk and blend again until it reaches your desired thickness.
  6. Chill the Dessert: Spoon the pudding into small serving bowls and place them in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to set and cool.
  7. Serve and Enjoy with toppings: Remove the pudding from the fridge and toss a few fresh berries or chocolate chips over the top before serving.
